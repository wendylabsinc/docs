Uses mDNS and BLE to discover nearby [WendyOS](../../wendyos/), [Wendy-Agent](../../wendy-agent/) and [Wendy Lite](../../wendy-lite/) devices. A device picker is shown in the terminal, similar to [Device Selection](../device-selection.md).

Pressing `u` will update the highlighted device to the latest [Agent](../../../wendy-agent/).

Pressing `d` will make the highlighted entry the [default device](../device-selection.md).
Pressing `x` will unset the default from the highlighted entry.

This menu shows your device name, available routes (BLE, LAN, Ethernet, ...) and Wendy-Agent version.
